About

My journey into web design and frontend development began with a strong curiosity about how websites work. As a visual thinker, I was always fascinated by how design and functionality come together to create seamless online experiences. This interest led me to pursue a course in web design and frontend development, where I discovered my passion for both the creative and technical aspects of building websites. Throughout the course, I immersed myself in learning the core principles of web design—understanding color theory, typography, and layout design, all while keeping the user’s perspective in mind. I also delved into frontend development, where I learned to bring those designs to life with clean, responsive code using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. The process of turning static designs into interactive, dynamic websites was incredibly rewarding, and it motivated me to continue exploring and refining my skills. The course was a perfect blend of creativity and logic, and it provided me with a solid foundation in both design and development. I worked on various projects that challenged me to think critically and push my boundaries. With every project, I gained a deeper understanding of how to create user-friendly, visually engaging websites that are not only beautiful but also functional. This journey has been a transformative experience, and it has equipped me with the tools and confidence to take on real-world projects. I’m excited to continue growing in this field, staying on top of the latest trends and technologies, and creating web experiences that make a lasting impact.

Let’s create something beautiful and functional together.

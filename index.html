About Me

Hi, I’m Jerry, a passionate frontend developer with a knack for building user-centric, interactive web experiences. With a strong background in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript frameworks like React, I thrive in fast-paced environments and love turning ideas into functional, beautiful websites.
